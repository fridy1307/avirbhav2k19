Egg Drop Challenge

Intro

Egg drop challenge is an on spot event in which teams have to design a compact structure using different types of materials acquired by them via an auction. Teams have to drop the structure from a fixed height. The aim is to prevent the egg from cracking.

Event Structure

Online disclosure of materials for the structure. Teams are required to decide on their design and choice of materials keeping in mind the fixed amount ₹100 provided to them.
Eggs will be provided next day
1hr shall be allotted for making the structure.
After an hour, the structures of each team shall be submitted and tested on next day.

Rules:

  1. Maximum size of structure – 12*12*12(inch)
  2. Fixed height – 20 feet (Round 1) | 30 feet (Round 2)
  3. Maximum of 5 members per team is allowed.
  4. Use of adhesives between Material and Egg is not permitted.
  5. 1 hour will be provided for gathering the materials
  6. Maximum time will be 1 hour for designing the structure
  7. The structure should be dropped with zero velocity.
  8. Two chances shall be given to each Team. However, the second chance will lead to a penalty of 10 points.
  9. Teams are not allowed to take the structure with them.
  10. Teams can only use material provided at that time through Auctioning. Using materials from outside will lead to disqualification.

Judging Criteria

The model will be evaluated based on its effectiveness, simplicity, design explanation, aesthetics and time to design the model. There will be some penalty in case the model doesn’t work in the first attempt.

Grading Policy (out of xxx points)

  • The model will be graded only if the egg remains intact or is just partially cracked (content of the egg doesn’t come out).
  • Partially Cracked Egg = 30 points
  • Intact Egg = 50 points
  • Simple & Effective design = 10 points
  • Design explanation = 10 points
  • -10 points for using the second chance.

Materials Available

All the participating groups will be given an equal amount of virtual money worth Rs.100 which they will use in buying the materials they are planning to use.

Egg Drop Challenge

Intro

Egg drop challenge is an on spot event in which teams have to design a compact structure using different types of materials acquired by them via an auction. Teams have to drop the structure from a height of 40ft. The aim is to prevent the egg from cracking.

Event Structure

Online disclosure of materials for the structure. Teams are required to decide on their design and choice of materials keeping in mind the fixed virtual money provided to them.
On the day of event teams will be required to submit their material requirements. 1hr shall be allotted for making the structure.
After an hour, the structures of each team shall be tested.

Rules:

  1. Maximum size of structure – 12*12*12(inch)
  2. Fixed height – 20 feet (Round 1) | 30 feet (Round 2)
  3. Maximum of 5 members per team is allowed.
  4. Use of adhesives between Material and Egg is not permitted.
  5. Maximum time will be 1 hour for designing the structure after getting material from auctioning.
  6. The structure should be dropped with zero velocity.
  7. Two chances shall be given to each Team. However, the second chance will lead to a penalty of 10 points.
  8. Teams are not allowed to take the structure with them.
  9. Teams can only use material provided at that time through Auctioning. Using materials from outside will lead to disqualification.

Judging Criteria

The model will be evaluated based on its effectiveness, simplicity, design explanation, aesthetics and time to design the model. There will be some penalty in case the model doesn’t work in the first attempt.

Grading Policy (out of xxx points)

  • The model will be graded only if the egg remains intact or is just partially cracked (content of the egg doesn’t come out).
  • Partially Cracked Egg = x points
  • Intact Egg = x points
  • Simple & Effective design = x points
  • Design explanation = x points
  • Time taken - <30 min = x points, <45 min = x points
  • Aesthetics = x points
  • -10 points for using the second chance.
  • Weightage will be higher for structures with the lowest amount of virtual money used.

Materials Available

All the participating groups will be given an equal amount of virtual money worth Rs.100 which they will use in buying the materials they are planning to use.

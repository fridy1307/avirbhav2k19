आर या पार
(Hindi Debate)

There are two rounds . One includes preliminary round and the other one is final round.

Rules for Preliminary round :

  1. Maximum 5 participants are allowed from each house.
  2. Each house will be given a particular slot for preliminary round.
  3. A topic will be given on spot and you have to speak for 2 minutes in favour or against of the topic.
  4. Top 8 participants will be selected for the final round.
  5. Decisions of the Judges will be treated as final.
  6. Completes rules for preliminary round will be posted on the facebook page of AVIRBHAV before start of the event.
  7. Rules for final round will be updated after the preliminary round.

Prime Time
(English Debate)

Rules:

  1. This is an online event where participants have to participate individually.
  2. Participants have to get themselves involved in a discussion over a given topic.
  3. Each topic will be active for 1 day only through the Avirbhav page ( starting time will be informed before the event starts).
  4. Each participant much choose a side (for or against the topic) at the beginning of the discussion. They are not allowed to change their standpoint at any further stage of the discussion.
  5. Participants may reply to a comment in case they oppose it but they must not deviate from the issue raised in the comment. The initial commenter may defend his/her opinion but must not raise any new issue in between the argument.
  6. Extra marks will be awarded to the participant who posts the first comment.
  7. At the end of the day participants may share a concluding argument. A participant who has concluded is not allowed to discuss further on the topic.
  8. The concluding argument should be in the following format

    Conclusion:
  9. Each comment must not exceed 150 words.
  10. Participants can use Hindi, English or Hinglish.
  11. "NO PLAGIARISM" 12. Comments must not violate the decorum of the forum.

काव्यांजलि
(Hindi Poetry)

Go ahead n take your heart out....
Poetry is the best way to convey what you have in your heart….

Rules:

  1. The poems can be of any genre like classic Kavita, shaayari(ghazals).
  2. The poem should be written in Hindi.
  3. The topic of the poem can be anything EXCLUDING controversial topics based on political parties, religion and obscenity.
  4. Plagiarism is strictly prohibited and if found will lead to disqualification straight away.
  5. Judgement will be based on the rhyme, use of figure of speech, issue being raised.
  6. Judge’s decision will be final.
  7. An individual can post maximum of 2 poems on event page

War of Mighty
(Story Writing)

Story telling is an effective way to communicate. The art of writing has the ability to draw us in, to inspire us, to make connections and to make us equal, understood and whole. Everyone has a story to tell.
Avirbhav brings you an opportunity to tell your tale. Let your pen be your sword and words your armor in this battle.

Rules:

  1. This is an individual event. No restrictions on number of entries.
  2. Participants shall be provided with rough sheets.
  3. Participants are required to bring their own pen/pencils.
  4. Frame of story shall be given on spot.
  5. Time allotted to each participant is 1.5 hour.
  6. Your story should follow the frame given on spot. Story should have an introduction, body and conclusion. Plot twist in story is encouraged.

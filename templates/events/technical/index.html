The Black Walker

Winter is coming!

You are a secret spy beyond the wall, keeping an eye on the white walkers and army of dead. A few days ago you noticed that the army of dead is on the rise.

Your job is to alert people on the wall. You have exhausted all other resources. All that is left is your pet black horse (the black Walker). You need to reach the other side before it’s too late……

Event Description

The challenge of the competition is to make a robot that can move on black lines on a white background and reach the finish as soon as possible. The ROBOT which will start on the START tile and reach the FINISH tile block by following the black lines in the shortest time.

Rules:

  1. A team of at most 4 and at least 2 is allowed.
  2. Robot should fulfill the given description of size.
  3. Only one team is allowed to run on the track at a time.
  4. Calibration time will be given to each team before start off run.
  5. Event shall be concluded in 2 rounds. First is the qualifier round. Time shall be the judge for you. Round 2 or the final round details shall be disclosed later.
  6. Each team should get ready in given time for run, failing can cause disqualification.
  7. Human interventions are allowed but will lead to deduction of points.
  8. Bots will undergo authenticity check , the participant should not dismantle their bot before completion of any round.
  9. Hard coding will lead to immediate disqualification.
  10. Decision made by Organizer shall be treated as final.

Arena:

Black lines of width 3cm on white background. A sample arena has been shown below. The real arena would be disclosed at the time of the competition

Robot Specifications:

  1. Each team is allowed to have only one autonomous bot.
  2. The bot must fit into cuboids of __x__x__cm {Length x Breadth x Height}.
  3. The bot should not communicate with any other devices. Data transfer violation will lead to disqualification.

Circuitous woods

Hold on your flags to encounter the victory by engaging yourself into the design challenge. It’s time to exhilarate the grey cells and put them for a test

Event Description

This event will be conducted in two rounds. First round (eliminative) tests the participant’s basic knowledge in electronics. This is a pen and paper round with multiple choice questions.

Round 2 is design challenge round. Qualified teams have to design circuit on PCB based on the lot they pick. Components to design the circuit have to be acquired by the team via auctioning using virtual money.

Rules:

Round 1:

  1. A team must consist of two participants only. No restrictions on number of teams from each house. Maximum participation is encouraged.
  2. Round 1 is eliminative round. This round will be of 1 hour.
  3. Time constrains is considered during evaluation.
  4. Use of unfair means will lead to disqualification

Round 2

  1. Each team has to select one problem statement from the given lot.
  2. Required components can be purchased via virtual money provided to each team.
  3. Output of circuit will be tested on CRO.
  4. Each team will be given 5 min prior auctioning of components to decide the components they require for the respective circuit.
  5. Each team shall be allotted 1 hour for designing the circuit and displaying the output.
  6. Each team is required to have own soldering iron and other required tools.
  7. Details of components and auctioning will be disclosed later.
  8. In all cases the decision of the judge will be final and binding

Quizzical Monsters

"Unlocking knowledge at the speed of thought"

Today’s modern life is surrounded with tech products. The technology has been growing rapidly for quite some time, and has now become an important part of life. It’s time to exhibit your technical knowledge and skills.

Event Description:

This event will be conducted in two rounds. First round (eliminative) tests the participant basic knowledge in computer science, electronics and quantitative aptitude. This is pen paper round with multiple choice questions.

Second round is stage round that further consists of three rounds.

Rules:

ELIMINATION ROUND –

  1. Each team would be given a set of question paper containing 30 multiple choice objective type questions
  2. Time limit - 60 minutes
  3. Only 8 Teams would be selected for STAGE ROUNDS.
  4. The selected teams shall have to appear for the final round.
  5. Use of unfair means will lead to disqualification.

FINAL QUIZ: STAGE ROUNDS

Round 1 : Tap Round

  1. A question will be fired & the team who tap the desk first get to answer first .
  2. 20 marks for the correct answer and negative 10 marks for the wrong or if team doesn’t after they tapping the desk. – No forwarding.

Round 2: Rapid Fire Round

  1. Each team will be asked 10 questions one after another.
  2. 10 marks for the correct answer and negative 5 marks for the wrong answer
  3. Answering time is only 5 seconds .
  4. Team discussion is allowed
  5. If a team cannot answer the question, they can say pass for the next question. The question will not be forwarded to the next team.

Round 3: Visual round

  1. Teams will be displayed picture and will have to answer.
  2. 10 marks for the correct answer and negative 5 marks for the wrong answer
  3. Each Team would be asked visual questions. ( 1 slide in each round )
  4. Answering time is only 90 seconds.
  5. No passing to the next team. Audience can answer.

Many Faced God

This competition is combination of crown creation and mask making.

A crown is most often resembles royalty but it also takes on a symbolic meaning of strength, courage, and respect. And mask covers your reality.

Rules:

  • A team should consist of 2 members.
  • Only 3 teams are allowed from each group.
  • For mask making Mask will be provided to you at the spot.
  • For crown chart paper and cardboard will be provided and rest should be arranged by participants.
  • Crown making should be based on “battle of throne” theme.
  • Allotted time will be 1hour.
  • Judgment will be based on creativity of both the art work.

Double crossing

This competition is combination of painting and collage making. Two members will be doing painting and another two members will be doing collage making. After sometime they will be swapped.

Rules:

  • A team should consist of 4members.
  • Only 3 teams are allowed from each group.
  • Chart papers and newspapers will be provided to the participants.
  • Any other requirements will be participants responsibility.
  • Allotted time will be 1 hour.
  • Judgment will be based on the beauty of both the creativity.

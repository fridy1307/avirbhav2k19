Vanity Fair

Rangoli is a fabulous hand drawing art of India. It is a way to express imagination.

Rules:

  • A team should consist of 4 members.
  • Only 1 team is allowed from each kingdom.
  • Rangoli art should be of 4ft x 4ft
  • Maximum time to complete the Rangoli is two hours.
  • Teams will be responsible for arrangement of the material required to prepare Rangoli.
  • Judgment will be based on appearance and color combination of your art.

1. Kissed by Fire
    (Solo dance)

    Display your individual brilliance, be the star of the stage that’s all yours.

    RULES-

    • From each team minimum of two participants are required and maximum limit is of three participants.
    • Time limit is 2-3:30 mins.
    • Participants are allowed to use props. Extra points will be awarded for the same.
    • The participants are requested to submit their track one day prior the event to the coordinator before 6 p.m., otherwise the participant will be disqualified.
    • The decision of judges will be final.

  2. Two Swords
    (Duet dance)

    Two is the best company. Choose the most compatible partner, and deliver a power pack performance with the best chemistry.

    RULES-

    • From each team minimum of 2 entries and max 3 entries will be entertained.
    • Time limit is 2-3 mins.
    • The participants are requested to submit their tracks one day prior the event to the coordinator before 6 p.m. otherwise the participant will be disqualified.
    • The decision of judges will be final.

  3. The dance of dragons
    ( Group dance)

    It is not about having best dancers. It is all about having the best team.

    RULES-

    • One group per team is required.
    • A group shall be made up of at least 4 members.
    • Time limit is 3-4 mins.
    • The participants are requested to submit their track one day prior the event to the coordinator before 6 p.m., otherwise the participant will be disqualified.
    • The decision of judges will be final.
    • Theme for group dance is FUSION. For further queries feel free to contact the event coordinators.
    • The participants will be judged on the basis of their rhythm, formations, synchronization and most importantly on the way they use the given theme (FUSION).

  4. You win or you die
    ( Face off )

    This is your chance to free yourself and express the best.

    RULES-

    • One participant per team is allowed.
    • Participants are requested to inform their names and their dance style to the coordinators before 2-3 days.
    • A list of 4-5 songs will be provided to the participants on the spot and they have to choose one song from that to perform.
    • Time limit is 1–1.30 mins.
    • The decision of judges will be final
